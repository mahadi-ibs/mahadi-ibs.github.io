PT. Logindo Samudra Makmur

PT Logindo Samudra Makmur is an Indonesian company specializing in the marine services sector, particularly providing offshore support vessels (OSV) for the oil and gas industry. Established in 1995, the company is a key player in Indonesia's maritime industry, serving both domestic and international clients. PT. Istana Berkat Sejahtera's project portfolio with PT Logindo Samudra Makmur includes:

2020 - Predictive Maintenance with Vibration Sensors

PT. Istana Berkat Sejahtera supported Logindo in implementing online condition monitoring using vibration sensors. This allowed Logindo to apply predictive maintenance, ensuring they could prepare necessary spare parts and avoid vessel downtime. The initiative resulted in cost savings of up to USD 10,000 per vessel annually.

2022 - Bearing Standardization

PT. Istana Berkat Sejahtera assisted PT. Logindo Samudra Makmur in switching from multiple bearing brands to a prominent, high-quality German bearing brand. This standardization simplified PT. Logindo Samudra Makmur's procurement process, improved price negotiation, and maintained high quality.
