About Company

Deliver inovative Industrial solution

Founded in 2020, PT Istana Berkat Sejahtera has quickly established itself as a prominent supplier of various industrial products in Indonesia. Specializing in hydraulic breakers, bearings and seals, sand machine components, air compressors, and engine parts, the company is committed to delivering high-quality and reliable solutions to meet the demands of Indonesia's growing industrial sector.

Product Range & Quality Commitment

PT Istana Berkat Sejahtera offers products that meet stringent industrial standards, ensuring durability and reliability across a wide range of applications. Their product portfolio includes:

  • Hydraulic Breakers - Powerful and efficient for construction and mining industries.
  • Bearings & Seals - Comprehensive range suitable for various mechanical systems, ensuring smooth operations.
  • Sand Machine & Accessories - High-quality parts for sand-making equipment, crucial for the construction industry.
  • Air Compressors & Engine Parts - Critical components for industrial machinery, offering long-lasting performance.

Expert Team & Engineering Support

The company is backed by a highly experienced team of engineers, many of whom have extensive expertise gained from working with global manufacturers and industry leaders. This technical knowledge enables PT Istana Berkat Sejahtera to provide expert guidance and after-sales support to its clients, ensuring that they receive the best possible solutions tailored to their specific needs.

Strategic Presence in Indonesia

Headquartered in Jakarta, PT Istana Berkat Sejahtera has built a strong network of sales and engineering teams located in major cities across Indonesia. This geographic reach allows the company to offer timely product delivery and responsive customer service, making it a trusted partner for clients nationwide.
